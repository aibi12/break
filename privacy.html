App: 1% Method — AI-Powered Fitness Coaching

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how 1% Method ("the App," "we," "our," or "us") handles your information. 1% Method is developed by Ardin, a sole proprietor based in Switzerland.

We built 1% Method with a privacy-first approach. The App stores all your data locally on your device. We do not operate servers, collect analytics, or track your behavior. We have no ability to access your data.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. All data generated by the App stays on your device.

The following data is created and stored locally on your device:

Data Type Storage Location Purpose
Checklist & routine data SwiftData (on device) Daily task tracking
Streak history SwiftData (on device) Motivation & progress tracking
Training plan configuration SwiftData (on device) Personalized workout plans
Hydration logs SwiftData (on device) Water intake tracking
HealthKit data (steps, workouts, water) Apple HealthKit (on device) Fitness integration
Subscription status Managed by Apple Access to premium features

We do not have access to any of this data. There is no server, no backend, no database, and no account system. We cannot see, retrieve, or analyze your information.

3. HealthKit Data

1% Method integrates with Apple HealthKit to:

  • Read: Step count data
  • Write: Workout sessions, water intake logs

HealthKit data is governed by Apple's strict HealthKit guidelines:

  • HealthKit data is never transmitted off your device by the App.
  • HealthKit data is never shared with third parties.
  • HealthKit data is never used for advertising or marketing.
  • HealthKit data is never sold to anyone.

You control HealthKit permissions through your device's Settings > Health > 1% Method. You can revoke access at any time.

4. AI Coaching & the Anthropic API

1% Method uses the Anthropic Claude API to power AI coaching features. Here is how this works:

  • The API key is managed by the Developer — you do not need to provide one.
  • When you use AI features, the App sends a prompt containing your fitness context (routine, preferences, progress) from your device to Anthropic's servers.
  • The AI-generated response is returned to your device and stored locally.

What is sent to Anthropic:

  • A text prompt containing your fitness preferences, goals, and plan parameters as you configured them in the App

What is NOT sent to Anthropic:

  • Your name or identity
  • Your HealthKit data
  • Your location
  • Your device identifiers

Anthropic's data handling: The data sent to Anthropic is subject to Anthropic's own privacy policy and terms. We encourage you to review them:

  • Anthropic Privacy Policy: anthropic.com/privacy
  • Anthropic API Terms: anthropic.com/api-terms

We are not responsible for how Anthropic processes data sent to their API. By using the AI coaching features, you acknowledge that your prompts are processed by Anthropic under their policies.

5. Subscriptions & Payments

1% Method offers auto-renewable subscriptions (monthly CHF 6.90, yearly CHF 44.90) with a 7-day free trial, managed entirely by Apple through the App Store.

  • We do not process payments. Apple handles all billing.
  • We do not receive or store your payment information, credit card details, Apple ID, or any financial data.
  • Subscription management, renewal, and cancellation are handled through your Apple ID settings.

For Apple's payment and privacy practices, refer to: apple.com/legal/privacy

6. Analytics, Tracking & Third-Party SDKs

None. 1% Method contains:

  • No analytics frameworks
  • No tracking pixels or identifiers
  • No third-party SDKs (other than Apple's native frameworks and the Anthropic API client)
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No cookies
  • No fingerprinting
  • No crash reporting services that transmit data externally

We do not know how many users we have, how often you open the App, or what features you use.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

All App data is stored locally on your device. Data retention is entirely under your control:

  • Delete specific data: Use in-app features to clear individual records.
  • Delete all data: Use the "Delete All Data" button in the App's settings. This permanently and irreversibly removes all App data from your device.
  • Delete the App: Uninstalling 1% Method removes all locally stored SwiftData. Note that HealthKit data written by 1% Method (workouts, water logs) remains in Apple Health unless you manually delete it there.
  • iCloud backup: If enabled, your plan configuration is backed up to iCloud Key-Value Storage tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on a new device.

Since we do not store any data on servers, there is nothing for us to delete on our end.

8. Data Security

  • All App data is stored in SwiftData on your device, protected by your device's passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID.
  • Communication with the Anthropic API uses HTTPS (TLS encryption) in transit.

9. Children's Privacy

1% Method is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly process data from children. Since the App does not collect data or require accounts, there is no mechanism for children to submit personal information to us.

If you believe a child is using the App inappropriately, please contact us.

Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP / DSG)

As a Swiss-based developer, we comply with the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (Datenschutzgesetz, DSG), revised version effective September 1, 2023.

EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

To the extent the GDPR applies to you, we respect the following rights:

Because 1% Method does not collect, store, or process personal data on any server, most traditional data protection rights are satisfied by design:

Right How 1% Method Addresses It

The App does not transfer your data to our servers (we have none). The only external data transfer occurs when you use AI plan generation, which sends prompts to Anthropic's servers (located in the United States). This transfer is initiated by you and governed by Anthropic's privacy policy.
